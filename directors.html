OUR MOTTO

LOVE LEARN LEAD
Motivated by the love of the Lord , Infinity Quest Academy seeks to educate children so that they may grow in knowledge and wisdom, live in humility and integrity, and serve God and mankind with joy and sincerity and Lead in the present day world.

Empowering learners, shaping leaders.
Empowering learners is our foundation, and shaping leaders is our vision. We are dedicated to equipping our students with the skills, values, and resilience needed to make a lasting impact in their lives and the world around them.
