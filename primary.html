Admissions offered for the Academic Year 2023-2024

Infinity Quest Academy is offering admissions to Grade Playgroup to UKG subject to the availability of seats and eligibility.

Submission of Application Form

The duly completed application form must be submitted at the School Office on or before the stipulated date. The following documents should accompany the form:

  • One certified copy of Child's Birth Certificate
  • Copy of Aadhar Card of the Child
  • Copy of Aadhar Card of Parents
  • Residential Address Proof (In case the address on the Aadhar card is not same as the current one)
  • Certified copy of School Report of the previous academic year (if applicable)
  • Three passport size photographs, two stamp size photographs of the child
  • Two passport size photographs of each parent.
  • Original Transfer Certificate from Previous School
  • Original documents of the submitted photocopies should be made available upon request for verification
  • The mere submission of an application form does not guarantee admission to the school

Confirmation of Admission

On receipt of acceptance of application form, parents are required to pay the annual fee within the stipulated date to block the seat. The school admission team shall be in touch with the parents to be connected and provide all necessary information as on when required. The parents will be informed about the Parent Orientation date and to pick up the stationary and books from the school.
